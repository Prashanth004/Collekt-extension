Instruction to use:

1) Click on the icon on top right f chrome window to get started.
2) You will need to sign up using google account.
3) Once signed in browse accross Social media. Type the reason in "why" field.
4) To see the cards collected click on the extension icon and then cards button.


What it does?

1) As you browse through your social media, a popup appears asking for a reason for you to contact the person whose profile you are viewing.
2) Stores the details of the person you contacted with the reason you had mentioned and the time you viewed in a beautiful card format.
3) Allows you to go through all the cards collected with a smart search box and necessary filters.
4) Stickers can attached to person describing them.
	Stickers available:
	1. A - active
	2. C - communicative
	3. H - helpful
(We are coming up with more appealing stickers, if you know any designers refer them)
5) The reason can be edited any time.
6) A list of name you desired can be created and you can add relevant people (cards) under that list.
7) The lists and cards can be deleted.

How is it useful?

1) The cards collected by this extension acts like a digital card which can be accessed any time.
2) Use-cases like searching for certain professionals, creators, experts, employees, employers can be served.
3) Lists could be used very well to have a group similar people can visit their profiles any time needed.

Example use cases:

1) I can collect all the card of models in Bangalore south for my Add shoot this month. I can group those under three lists as 
	a) models who stay within 5 miles 
	b) Models with very strong profile.
	c) models who are free on 30/12/2018

2) React-JS developer under three lists
	a) Fresher
	b) Experienced
	c) Freelancers
